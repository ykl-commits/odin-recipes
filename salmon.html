Broiled Salmon With Mustard and Lemon

In this simple salmon recipe, a quick stin under the broiler
transforms smooth Dijon mustard into a savory, caramalized crust,
and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice adds just the right brighness
to the rich, sweet fish.

Ingredien

  • 2 (6- to 8-ounce) skin-on salmon fillets, each about 1-inch thick
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
