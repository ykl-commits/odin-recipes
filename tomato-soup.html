Tomato Soup

Quick Tomato Soup With Grilled Cheese

When done well, this age-old combination can be exactly what you need.
Here, the cooking is as easy sa the eating: While you get the soup started
in a pot with a can of tomatoes and little more than onion butter (in the
style of Marcella Hazan's famous tomato sauce), you assemble and pan-fry
the sandwiches.

Ingredents

  • 1 (28-ounch) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth, plus more as needed
  • 1 large yellow onion, coursely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
