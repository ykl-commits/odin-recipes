Steak Diane for Two

Though you can follow this procedure with almost any tender cut of beef
(and with chicken breasts, if that direction appeals to you), it's a perfect
treatment for tenderloin medallions (filet mignon).

Ingredents

  • 2 (6-ounce) beef fillets
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced shallot or onion
